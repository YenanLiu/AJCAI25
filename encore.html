Call for Papers: Encore Track

To highlight the impactful and latest research in machine learning and artificial intelligence, we are pleased to introduce the Encore Track at our upcoming conference. This track is designed to disseminate significant works that have already been presented at other prestigious venues and to foster further interactions and discussions among researchers.

Researchers are invited to submit an abstract, title, and author information for recent publications that have been published, are in press, or have been accepted since June 1, 2024. Suitable venues include, but are not limited to, top-tier conferences and journals in the field such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ECCV, ICCV, CVPR, ACL, AAAI, JMLR and IEEE TPAMI.

Presentation Format: Accepted submissions will be presented in the encore track during the main conference week of events.

Review Process: The review process will be carried out immediately after the submission. The acceptance of each submission will be determined based on the quality and relevance of the research topic. To further enrich the discussions and insights shared at the conference, we hope that accepted submissions not only meet high scientific standards but also bring additional diversity and breadth to the conference program.

Submissions: To submit your work, you will need to include:

  • A PDF of the published paper.
  • The official URL linking to the published version.
  • For papers accepted but not yet published, evidence of acceptance (e.g., acceptance email) should also be submitted in PDF format.

Authors should submit the required information via this Google Form (link will be updated soon).

Important Dates:

  • Submission Deadline: TBD 2025 (23:59 AEST, UTC+10)
  • Notification of Acceptance: Within 7 days of submission
